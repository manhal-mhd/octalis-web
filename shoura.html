Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right mentor?

Browse mentor profiles, read reviews, and book a free introduction call to find the perfect match for your goals.

What's included in a mentorship session?

Each session includes one-on-one guidance, actionable feedback, and resources tailored to your needs.

How long are the sessions?

Most sessions are 60 minutes long, with flexibility for 30-minute check-ins or 90-minute deep dives.

Can I change my mentor?

Yes, you can switch mentors at any time to ensure you're getting the most value from your mentorship.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment?

Mentors typically spend 2-4 hours per week, while mentees can choose their preferred session frequency.

Is there a cost involved?

Mentorship sessions are currently offered free of charge to support our mission of accessible education.

How are mentors and mentees matched?

We use a careful matching process based on industry, skills, goals, and mutual availability.
